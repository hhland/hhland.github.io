Description of how to create a Pareto Chart using Minitab  

Scenario

A clothing manufacturer has had numerous returns due to various quality complaints. The manufacturer wants to correct the problems but isn't sure where to begin. They decide to track the number and type of defects in their clothing line. Identifying the sources of their most common problems will help them to target their improvement efforts.

Choose the appropriate analysis

The manufacturer wants to determine the most common sources of fabric defects. A Pareto chart is appropriate for this task.
  1. Open the worksheet CLOTHINGDEFECT.MTW.
  2. Choose Stat > Quality Tools > Pareto Chart.

Enter your data

Instead of a long list of defects, this worksheet contains a short table of defect names and frequencies.

  1. In Defects or attribute data in, enter Defect.

  2. In Frequencies in, enter Count.
  3. Click OK.

Specify options

This pareto chart requires no further modifications, but other options are available. You can assign names to the chart and axes, and suppress the cumulative percent line with the Options subdialog box.
  • (no action required)
