Data requirements for I-MR chart  

What kind of data is required?

You can have only one numeric measure per control chart. By definition, data for an I-MR chart is not divided into subgroups because you cannot reasonably collect it this way; the process may occur infrequently, or more regular sampling may be very difficult or expensive. For example:
  • Weekly scrap totals at a granite processing facility
  • Grinding time of large telescope lenses
  • Failure speed of a jet engine
  • Assembly time for a modular home
Note If you can collect your data in subgroups, it's generally good to do so. Xbar-R and Xbar-S charts are similar to the I-MR chart, but they require subgroups.
