Data requirements for Xbar-R chart  

What kind of data is required?

You must consider only one numeric measure per control chart. For example:
  • Tensile strength of copper wire
  • Fill weight of cereal boxes
  • Time to change oil
  • Your blood pressure
Consider the size, frequency, and number of subgroups to be charted. Your sample size (subgroup size) must be at least 2 and as large as 8. (If your sample size is larger than 8, use an Xbar-S Chart.) To be representative of the process, all of samples that you take must be within a subgroup at the same time. For example:
  • Measure the tensile strength for 3 pieces of wire every 4 hours
  • Measure the fill weight for 5 boxes of cereal each hour
  • Select 5 work orders and determine how long workers needed to change out the oil
  • Take 2 blood pressure measurements each day at the same time

Choose subgroups so that differences among measurements within the same subgroup are small and so you can detect differences between subgroups. For initial process studies, subgroups of 4 or 5 measured every hour or so are typical. As the process demonstrates stability (or as improvements are made), you can decrease the subgroup size and frequency.

Collect subgroups for a duration that is long enough to assure that major sources of variation have the chance to occur. Usually, 25 or more subgroups is enough.

Generally, industry prefers small, frequent samples to signal a process shift before too much defective product is made.

What should my worksheet look like?

Samples in one column

Data are contained in one column, with subgroup identifiers in a second column.

Note If you have unequal subgroup sizes, you must use this layout.
C1 C2
Strength Subgroup
1 20.4 1
2 21.3 1
3 20.9 1
4 21.7 2
5 22.2 2
6 22.1 2

Samples in different rows

Data for each subgroup are in separate rows.

Subgroup Strength1 Strength2 Strength3

Samples in different rows

You can also use the date and time of the sample as a subgroup identifier. For example, subgroup 1 was taken 9/1 06:00 AM and subgroup 2 was taken 9/1 10:00 AM.
