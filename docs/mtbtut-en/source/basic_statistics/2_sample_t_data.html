Data requirements for 2-sample t.  

What kind of data is required?

You must have only two groups with continuous data. For example:

  • Compression strength for two brands of concrete
  • Cost of machine repairs from two service providers

If you have more than two groups, use an ANOVA procedure.

Your samples must be independent (measured on different subjects). For example:
  • Delivery times measured for two couriers
  • Hospital errors tracked on two different hospitals
  • Failure times measured for two tire brand

Consider how much data you need to give the test enough power to detect differences between groups. To help determine the sample size that you need, use Stat > Power and Sample Size > 2-Sample t.
