Description of how to perform a Normality Test using Minitab  

Scenario

A dietitian selects a random sample of 13 bottles of cooking oil to determine if the mean percentage of saturated fat is different from the advertised 15%. Previous research indicates that the population standard deviation is 2.6%. It seems appropriate to use a one-sample Z-test, but the assumption of normality needs to be verified.

Choose the appropriate analysis

The dietitian must verify whether her data are normal, so she uses Normality Test.
  1. Open the worksheet FAT.MTW.
  2. Choose Stat > Basic Statistics > Normality Test.

Enter your data

Enter a single column of data to test for normality.
  1. In Variable, enter 'Fat Content'.
  2. Click OK.

Specify options

This normality test requires no further modifications, but other options are available. Use the main dialog box to plot reference lines and choose among various normality tests.
  • (no action required)
