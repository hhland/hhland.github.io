Data requirements for Normality Test  

What kind of data is required?

Your data must be numeric. For example:
  • The wait time at an airport security checkpoint
  • Monthly revenue of a bookstore
  • The weight of steaks at a local restaurant
  • Daily mean temperature at the North Pole

What should my worksheet look like?

You must provide a single column of numeric data.
