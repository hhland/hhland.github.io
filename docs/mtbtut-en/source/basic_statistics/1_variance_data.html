Data requirements for 1 Variance  

What kind of data is required?

Data should be numeric, continuous, randomly collected measurements that follow any distribution. For example:
  • Time required to complete a survey
  • Weight of bags of apples
  • Diameter of hubcaps

Consider how much data you need to give the test enough power to detect differences between your population variance and a target value. To help determine the sample size you need, use Stat > Power and Sample Size > 1 Variance.

What should my worksheet look like?

Raw data

List every observation in a single column. If you enter multiple columns, Minitab performs multiple 1 Variance procedures.
