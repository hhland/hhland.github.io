Description of how to perform 2-Sample Poisson Rate using Minitab  

Scenario

You are an analyst for the postal service, and you want to compare two post office branches to determine which has the greater daily rate of customer visits. You count the number of customers who enter each branch for 40 business days (9:00 AMÂ–5:00 PM) and compare the numbers of visits to each branch with the 2-sample Poisson rate function.

Choose the appropriate analysis

The analyst wants to analyze the difference between rates of two Poisson processes. 2-Sample Poisson Rate is the appropriate test for this situation.
  1. Open the worksheet POSTOFFICE.MTW.
  2. Choose Stat > Basic Statistics > 2-Sample Poisson Rate.

Enter your data

This worksheet contains a column of raw data for each sample.
  1. Choose Each sample is in its own column.
  2. In Sample 1, enter 'Branch A'.
  3. In Sample 2, enter 'Branch B'.
  4. Click OK.

Specify options

This 2-sample Poisson rate analysis requires no further modifications, but other options are available. Use the Options subdialog box to request a one-sided test, or to change the confidence level, test difference, or length of observation for each sample.
  • (no action required)
