Description of how to a using Minitab  

Scenario

A chemical manufacturer wants to maximize the yield of a catalytic reaction. The researchers choose a central composite design to study the effects of time and temperature on the yield of the reaction. Because the data will be collected on two different days, the design contains two blocks.

Choose the appropriate design and number of factors

The researchers want to use a central composite design with 2 factors.
  1. Choose Stat > DOE > Response Surface > Create Response Surface Design.
  2. Under Type of Design, choose Central composite.
  3. In Number of factors, choose 2.
  4. Click Designs.
  5. In the box at the top, choose the full design with 14 runs.
  6. Click OK.

Name factors and define factor settings

You can name each factor and define the factor settings.
  1. Click Factors and complete the table as follows:
    Factor Name Low High
    A Time 80 100
    B Temp 140 150
  2. Click OK in each dialog box.

Interpreting the output
