Data requirements for factorial design plots.  

What kind of data is required?

Once you have analyzed your design, you are ready to create factorial, contour, and response surface plots.

What should my worksheet look like?

When you create a design, Minitab stores the standard order, run order, center point indicator, block assignment, and each factor in separate columns. Each row in the worksheet contains data that corresponds to one run of your experiment. After you conduct the experiment, enter the response data for each run. The exact makeup of your worksheet will vary depending on your design choices, factor information, and responses.
