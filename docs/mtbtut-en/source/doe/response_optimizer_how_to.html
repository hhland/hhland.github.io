Description of how to use response optimizer in Minitab  

Scenario

A building materials manufacturer is developing a new insulation product. They are interested in three quality characteristics: insulation value, density, and strength of the insulation.

Choose the appropriate analysis

The manufacturer has already analyzed a factorial design, so they are ready to use the Response Optimizer.
  1. Open Insulation2.MTW.
  2. Choose Stat > DOE > Factorial > Response Optimizer.

Specify the goal for each response

The manufacturer wants to optimize three responses: Insulation, Density, and Strength.
  1. For Strength, choose Target.
  2. For the target for strength, enter 30.
  3. For Density, choose Minimize.
  4. For Insulation, choose Maximize.

Specify the goal, boundaries, and importance for each response

You must specify a goal (minimize, target, or maximize) and boundaries for each response. The manufacturer wants to find the settings that maximize insulation value, meet a target strength, and minimize density. Optionally, you can define the shape of the desirability function or specify the comparative importance of the response.
  1. Click Setup and complete the table as follows:
    Response Goal Lower Target Upper Weight Importance
    Strength Target 20 30 40 0.5 1
    Density Minimize .5 .5 1 1 1
    Insulation Maximize 25 60 60 1 1
  2. Click OK in each dialog box.
