Description of how to perform a Gage R&R Study (Nested) using Minitab  

Scenario

Three operators each measured the temperature of three different parts three times. Note that the parts measured by each operator are unique.

Choose the appropriate analysis

Because each part is measured by only one operator, it is appropriate to use a Gage R&R Study (Nested) rather than a Gage R&R Study (Crossed).
  1. Open the worksheet TEMPERATURE.MTW.
  2. Choose Stat > Quality Tools > Gage Study > Gage R&R Study (Nested).

Enter your data

Enter the columns that contain the part or batch numbers, operators, and measurement data.
  1. In Part or Batch numbers, enter Part.
  2. In Operators, enter Operator.
  3. In Measurement data, enter Response.

Specify options

Many options are available with this gage R&R study. In this example, the historical standard deviation and process tolerance are known, so they should be entered in Options.
  1. Click Options.
  2. Under Process tolerance, choose Upper spec - Lower spec and enter 500.
  3. In Historical standard deviation, enter 87.3786.
  4. Click OK in each dialog box.
