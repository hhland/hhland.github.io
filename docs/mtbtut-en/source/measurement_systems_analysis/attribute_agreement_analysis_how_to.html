Description of how to perform an Attribute Agreement Analysis using Minitab  

Scenario

A fabric company uses appraisers to rate the print quality of cotton fabric on a 1-5 scale. To assess the consistency and correctness of the appraisers' ratings, four appraisers rated print quality on 30 samples of fabric twice. Fabric samples were randomly presented.

Choose the appropriate analysis

To evaluate this rating system, which consists of multiple trials by multiple appraisers, use Attribute Agreement Analysis.
  1. Open the worksheet CALICO.MTW.
  2. Choose Stat > Quality Tools > Attribute Agreement Analysis.

Enter your data

This worksheet contains columns of stacked data. The rating scale is ordinal, so you should check Categories of the attribute data are ordered.
  1. In Attribute column, enter Response.
  2. In Samples, enter Sample.
  3. In Appraisers, enter Appraiser.
  4. In Known standard/attribute, enter Standard.
  5. Check Categories of the attribute data are ordered.
  6. Click OK.

Specify options

This attribute agreement analysis requires no further modifications, but other options are available. To specify study information, click Information. To display the disagreement table, request Cohen's kappa, and change the confidence level, click Options. To specify graph options, click Graphs. To control the display of the output, click Results.
  • (no action required)
