Data requirements for Gage R&R Study (Nested)  

What kind of data is required?

Your measurement data must be numeric and continuous. For example:

  • Temperature of a metal in degrees Celsius
  • Speed of a car in miles per hour
  • Tensile strength of a wire

You also need a part name or part number column and a column for the operators. Parts and operators can be text or numbers. For example:

  • Parts A, B, C, and D and operators 1, 2, 3
  • Parts 111, 117, and 119 and operators Sam and Laura

In a Gage R&R Study (Nested) the part is nested within operator because each operator measures unique parts. Select parts that represent the actual or expected range of process variation.

If each part is measured by more than one operator, analyze the data using a Gage R&R Study (Crossed).
