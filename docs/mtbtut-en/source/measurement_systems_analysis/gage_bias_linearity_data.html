Data requirements for Gage Linearity and Bias Study  

What kind of data is required?

Your measurement data and reference values must be numeric and continuous. For example:
  • Weight of a steel rod in kilograms
  • Length of an automotive part in inches
  • Diameter of piston rings in centimeters
You also need a part name or number column that refers to each measurement. Parts can be text or numbers. For example:
  • Parts A, B, C, and D
  • Parts 111, 117, and 119

The Automotive Industry Action Group (AIAG) recommends choosing at least 5 parts that cover the operating range of the measuring device. Obtain at least 10 measurements for each part.
